Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sports Block Puzzle ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Local Data Storage:

Our app stores game data locally on your device using browser localStorage. This includes:

  • Your game scores and statistics
  • Journey mode progress (levels completed, stars earned)
  • Game settings and preferences (sound, vibration, voice)
  • Selected themes and customization options

No Personal Information:

We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information such as:

  • Name, email, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • User accounts or passwords

2. How We Use Your Information

All game data is stored locally on your device and is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress and high scores
  • Remember your game settings and preferences
  • Provide a personalized gaming experience
  • Track achievements and level completion

Your data never leaves your device and is not shared with any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Currently, our app operates completely offline and does not integrate with any third-party services. Future versions may include:

  • AdMob (Google): For displaying advertisements (if enabled)
  • Google Play Services: For in-app purchases (if enabled)

If these services are added, this policy will be updated accordingly, and you will be notified.

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures
  • We do not transmit data over the internet
  • There is no risk of data breaches from our servers (we don't have any)

Note: If you uninstall the app or clear browser/app data, all your game progress will be permanently deleted.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any personal data at all, parents can feel confident allowing their children to play.

6. Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • Vibration: To provide haptic feedback during gameplay (optional, can be disabled)
  • Storage: To save game progress locally on your device

These permissions are used only for their stated purposes and do not access any personal data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app (scores, statistics)
  • Delete: You can clear your data at any time through the app settings or by uninstalling the app
  • Export: Currently, there is no data export feature as all data is stored locally
